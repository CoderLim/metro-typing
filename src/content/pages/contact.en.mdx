## Contact Metro Typing

For questions, bug reports, content corrections, privacy requests, or partnership inquiries, email contact@metrotyping.org.

When reporting a gameplay issue, please include:

- The subway line and direction you selected
- Your browser and device
- What happened and what you expected to happen
- A screenshot if it helps explain the issue

We review messages as time allows and prioritize reports that affect availability, safety, privacy, or major gameplay problems.
