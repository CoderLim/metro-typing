## Email

For website questions, privacy requests, content corrections, or removal requests, email `contact@metrotyping.org`.

## What to include

Please include the page URL, a short description of the issue, and any context needed to reproduce or understand it. For privacy requests, include enough information for us to identify the relevant data without sending sensitive details unnecessarily.

## Response scope

This contact address is for metrotyping.org. Questions about the original game service at metrotyping.kr may need to be handled by that service directly.
