## What Metro Typing Does

Metro Typing is a browser game for practicing Hangul typing with subway station names. Instead of typing random words, you follow a subway line, recall the next stop, and type the station name accurately enough to keep moving.

The goal is simple: choose a line, keep your accuracy steady, and finish the route with a better time than your last run.

## Why It Exists

Typing practice is easier to stick with when the words mean something. Subway stations give each round a clear path, a familiar memory hook, and a small challenge that changes by line and direction.

Metro Typing is built for people who like transit maps, Korean language practice, casual browser games, or personal record challenges.

## Editorial Approach

The guides and articles on this site focus on practical typing habits, Hangul input setup, subway-line memory, and ways to review mistakes after a run. We aim to keep content clear, useful, and specific to Metro Typing rather than publishing unrelated filler.
