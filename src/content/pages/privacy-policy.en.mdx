## Introduction

Metro Typing (metrotyping.org) treats your privacy carefully. This policy explains what we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it.

## Information We Collect

This site can be used without creating an account. Information we may collect includes:

1. **Usage records** — anonymized or aggregated data such as pages visited, visit time, and approximate region, used to improve the service
2. **Device and browser information** — basic technical details used to optimize the experience
3. **Cookies and similar technologies** — small data files used to remember preferences, measure usage, prevent abuse, and support advertising where enabled

## How We Use Information

- Provide and improve the site
- Identify errors and security issues
- Meet legal obligations
- Measure site performance and understand which content is useful
- Serve or support advertising, when advertising is enabled

## Sharing with Third Parties

We do not sell personal information. We may share data only when required by law, or with trusted providers to the extent needed to operate the service.

## Advertising and Google Services

Metro Typing may use Google AdSense, Google Analytics, or similar third-party services. These providers may use cookies, web beacons, IP addresses, device identifiers, browser information, and page interaction data to serve ads, limit ad frequency, detect invalid traffic, measure ad performance, and provide analytics.

Third-party vendors, including Google, may place and read cookies in your browser or use web beacons because ads or measurement tools are served on this site. Google may use advertising cookies to personalize ads based on prior visits to this and other sites. You can learn about Google advertising data use in [Google's advertising technology policy](https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads) and manage personalization controls in [Google Ad Settings](https://adssettings.google.com/).

We do not intentionally send personally identifiable information such as email addresses, account passwords, or precise identity data to Google in ad requests.

## Your Choices

You can control cookies through your browser settings. If you are in a region that requires consent for personalized ads or analytics cookies, we will use available consent controls before enabling those features where required.

## Contact

For privacy questions, contact us at contact@metrotyping.org. This policy may be updated when needed.
