## Introduction

Metro Typing (metrotyping.org) treats your privacy carefully. This policy explains what we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it.

## Information We Collect

This site can be used without creating an account. Information we may collect includes:

1. **Usage records** — anonymized or aggregated data such as pages visited, visit time, and approximate region, used to improve the service
2. **Device and browser information** — basic technical details used to optimize the experience
3. **Cookies** — small data used to remember preferences and for analytics

The embedded original game (metrotyping.kr) may follow that service’s own policies.

## How We Use Information

- Provide and improve the site
- Identify errors and security issues
- Meet legal obligations

## Sharing with Third Parties

We do not sell personal information. We may share data only when required by law, or with trusted providers to the extent needed to operate the service.

## Google Advertising and Analytics

Metro Typing may use Google AdSense and Google Analytics. When these services are active, Google and its partners may use cookies, web beacons, IP addresses, device identifiers, browser details, and page interaction data to measure traffic, prevent abuse, serve ads, and improve ad quality.

Third parties, including Google, may place or read cookies in your browser or use web beacons because ads or analytics are served on this site. These technologies can be used for contextual ads, frequency capping, fraud prevention, reporting, and, where allowed, personalized ads.

You can learn more about how Google uses data on partner sites at [Google Privacy & Terms](https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites), review ad cookie details at [Google advertising technologies](https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads), and control personalized ads at [Google Ad Settings](https://adssettings.google.com/).

If you are in the EEA, the United Kingdom, or Switzerland, we will seek consent where required before using cookies or identifiers for personalized ads or analytics. You can withdraw or change consent through the available consent controls when they are shown.
