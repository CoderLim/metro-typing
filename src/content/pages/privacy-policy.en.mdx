## Introduction

Metro Typing (metrotyping.org) treats your privacy carefully. This policy explains what we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it.

## Information We Collect

This site can be used without creating an account. Information we may collect includes:

1. **Usage records** — anonymized or aggregated data such as pages visited, visit time, and approximate region, used to improve the service
2. **Device and browser information** — basic technical details used to optimize the experience
3. **Cookies** — small data used to remember preferences and for analytics

The embedded original game (metrotyping.kr) may follow that service’s own policies.

## How We Use Information

- Identify errors and security issues
- Meet legal obligations

## Sharing with Third Parties

We do not sell personal information. We may share data only when required by law, or with trusted providers to the extent needed to operate the service.
